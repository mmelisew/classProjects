About Us

Our team consists of a multicultural group that has lived all over the world and the United States. Now, all of us live in Chicago and are more than excited to share our favorite places and experiences with the world. We have enjoyed this city for many years from different perspectives and neighborhoods. Being Chicago such a rich city in culture, we strive to deliver the best coverage of the city.
