Food

Chicago Theatre

The most popular Chicago-style foods are the Chicago-style hot dog, which will traditionally be steamed or boiled, with yellow mustard, onion, tomato, green sweet-pickle relish, sport peppers, a dill pickle spear and celery salt--and never ketchup. You can find the best chicago style hot dogs at Portillo's.

Aragon Ballroom

There is also Chicago-style deep-dish pizza which has a tall otter crust with large amounts of cheese with chunky tomato sauce on top of cheese instead of underneath it. It is very similar to stuffed pizza, but has more cheese and a second thinner crust. That doesn't mean thin crust isn't also popular, but Chicago has become iconic for deep-dish style. The best places to get pizza within the city are Lou Malnati’s and Giordano’s. There is such a diverse culture of food within the area that Chicago even has its own food fest. Its called Taste of Chicago and is held in early July in Grant Park in downtown. It features booths from dozens of Chicago-area restaurants, as well as live music.
