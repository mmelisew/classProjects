Whether you have been there or not, the Chicago Theatre is stranger to none. Located downtown on Lake and State Avenue by the State train station, the Chicago Theatre hosts an endless array of performances every week. Not only does it host concerts for every kind of music, from classical to rock, but also plays, musicals, and more.

 

 

 

 

 

History

Some sections of the “L” have been operating since 1892, making it the second oldest rapid transit system in both Americas. The public CTA system is credited with fostering growth in the of Chicago’s dense populus and is one of Chicago's distinctive features. It consist of 8 rapid transit lines that is laid out in a spoke-hub distribution paradigm focused towards the loop. The “L” as some people call it, is short for elevated, but is also found underground, grade level, or open cut at some portions.
