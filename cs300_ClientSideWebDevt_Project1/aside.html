Aside tag in HTML5

Aside - Defines content aside from the page content.

  • It is new in HTML5
  • It is designed to provide more information to an article or a content.
  • It uses to put a section of a page that is related to the article.
  • It doesn't need to be part of the article. But it should be related to surrounding content.

Browsers that support aside tag are: chrome 6.0, internet explorer 9.0, mozilla 4.0, safari 5.0, opera 11.1.

It also supports global attributes in html.

Aside content doesn't have to be in the side. It can be in the middle, left side or right side.
